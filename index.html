Who we are

Moxhe fine pastries is the pop-up project of the team behind Moxhe restaurant. We have been running our seafood restaurant, at 65b Macpherson street Bronte, since 2015.


During the Covid lockdown we were presented with the opportunity to transform ourselves into a take-away pastry and dessert venue instead of our usual seafood offerings. Whilst the lockdowns came with many challenges, we found that we really enjoyed making pastries (and people seemed to really enjoy eating them!) and it started a fire in our bellies and a hope in our hearts to one day open pastry shop.


we produce small batches of cream, biscuits and chocolate but often, as opposed to one big batch once a day, to keep the flavours vibrant
